Upcoming Zion Referendum

The city of Zion will ask voters in November to grant it home-rule authority, which would be used to help address a projected budget shortfall and give the city more regulatory powers. Home-rule authority, which is automatically granted to cities with a population of at least 25,000, can also be secured through a voter referendum. It would allow the city to impose an additional salestax, and to raise property taxes faster than it otherwise would be able to under state law. Cypress Park is already paying high property taxes, we need to stop this madness. We cannot give the city anymore power over us. Let your friends and family know about the upcoming election. Please, when voting consider what is the best for your family and the Cypress Park community.

Neighborhood Watch

Recently we have had several homeowners vehicles broken into, again. Here are some things you can do to help protect your car from being burglarized:

  • Park your car inside your garage and turn on outside lights. This will not only keep you safe when you exit the car, it will also help keep your car safe because thieves tend to avoid areas that are highly visible.
  • Don’t leave your car unlocked. Make sure the doors are locked and the windows rolled all the way up. If you have a car alarm or steering wheel lock, use it. Even if you are only leaving your car for a minute, make sure it is locked.
  • Don’t leave valuables inside your car. Take purses, wallets, laptops, gym bags, etc with you. Even if you have nothing of value inside them, bags and briefcases are tempting targets.
  • Watch out for suspicious activity near your car. Trust your instincts, they are usually right. Keep an eye out for cars slowly cruising your neighborhood, or people looking into parked cars.

As always, if you see something suspicious or a crime in progress, call the Zion Police, 911 or non emergency at (847)872-8000
